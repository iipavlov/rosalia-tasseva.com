Awards

1974 - Diploma at The competition "Young musician" – Bulgaria

1978 - Honorary diploma at The International Competition "Usti nad Labem"-Czech Republic

1984 - Diploma at The Seventh Bulgarian Competition

1987 - First prize at The National Competition “The Golden Diana"-with piano-quartet

1998 - Nomination "The musician of the 1998" of The National Bulgarian Radio

2010 - Second prize as composer, author of the lyrics and music arranger for the creating of new children song at The First national music festival “The pink lion” in Varna

2013 - First prize on The International Music Festival “Sea and memories” in Varna, Bulgaria for new author pop song “Sea element” – music and lyrics Rosalia Tasseva, arranger Pavel Vassev, singer Julietta Ivanova from “The Voice of Bulgaria”
